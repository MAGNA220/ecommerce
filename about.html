It's just me

I'm Grace, an everyday individual with an exceptional passion for arts and crafts. Amidst my commitment to an 8-hour, 5-day-a-week job, I have embraced arts and crafts as not just a pastime but a therapeutic hobby. Engaging in creative pursuits serves as a rejuvenating escape after a bustling and demanding week. My artistic focus primarily revolves around the simplicity of drawings, with a special affection for sketching my beloved pets, various animals, and the beauty of nature, using both traditional pencils and colored pencils.

If my artistic endeavors bring a sense of joy, you have the opportunity to make them a permanent part of your surroundings. Each piece is not just a creation but a potential enhancement for your living or working space, infusing it with a unique and personal touch of art. By considering a purchase, you not only support my passion but also bring a piece of my artistic expression into your life, creating an environment that reflects both creativity and individuality. Your choice to acquire my artworks adds a meaningful and aesthetic dimension to your space, fostering a connection between the art and the beholder.

"With arts and crafts, I can create the kind of world in which I wish to live" -- Joseph Curiale
