Hello! My name is Circe Grace

I am just an ordinary person with an extraordinary love for arts. I have a job that requires me to work 8 hours a day, 5 days a week. Yet, I've embraced arts as my recreational pursuit and passion, serving as a means to unwind and alleviate stress after a demanding week. My drawings are simple. I love to draw my pets, animals, and nature using pencils, colored pencils and watercolor.

I also have a deep appreciation for crafts. The creations crafted by local artisans in our community never fail to impress me. I am eager to support and promote their products , which are lovingly handcrafted by our fellow kababayans.
